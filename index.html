Downtown Seattle's Rich Food History

Seattle is a bustling hub with a variety of international cuisiness accessible. It is popular with tourists and locals alike. Seafood is especially popular with Seatte's proximity to Puget Sound and the port of Seattle has the freshest seafood. There is a great deal of both cultural and ecological diversity in the city. Coffee can practically be considered a food group in and of itself, as Tully's, Seattle's Best, and Starbucks all call Seattle home.
